Common Questions

Questions we are often asked

A few things people like to know before they get in touch. If your question is not covered here, please call or email the office and we will be glad to help.

Where are you based?+
Our legal office is at 7 St Catherine Street, Cupar, Fife, KY15 4LS, in the centre of the town. Our estate agency office is a short walk away at 10 St Catherine Street.
What areas do you cover?+
We act for clients in Cupar and across North East Fife, including St Andrews, Newport-on-Tay, Tayport, Auchtermuchty, Ladybank, Falkland and Springfield, as well as the wider Fife area.
What services do you offer?+
We handle residential and commercial property and conveyancing, family law, wills, executries and powers of attorney, and court work and dispute resolution. You can read more about each area in the services section above.
Do you provide Notary Public services?+
Yes. Both of our directors, Douglas I G Gray and Kenneth W Gray, are Notaries Public, so we can attend to documents that require notarial certification here at the Cupar office.
How do I make an appointment?+
Call the office on 01334 656644, send an email to info@williamsgraywilliams.co.uk, or use the enquiry form below. A member of the firm will arrange a convenient time to speak with you.
Are you regulated?+
Yes. Williams Gray Williams is regulated by the Law Society of Scotland, and both directors are qualified solicitors and Notaries Public.
Do I need to come into the office to speak with you?+
You are welcome to visit us at St Catherine Street in Cupar. If it is easier for you, we can also discuss your matter by telephone or email and arrange to meet when it suits you.
